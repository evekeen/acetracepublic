It's easy to trace your throw in a video

  • Put your phone in the holder
  • Film your throw
  • Use the Ace Trace app on your iOS device
  • Create a line that highlights your throw
  • Mention @acetrace.app in your posts and we will repost the best shots
    •

    If you don't have the app or an iOS device, follow these steps so we can create a line for you:

  • Open a video on your phone
  • Tap "Share" button
  • Choose "Copy iCloud Link" or "Create Link" - depends on your device
  • Send an email with a link to video@acetrace.app
  • Give us some time to draw the line for you and we will share it back
    •
