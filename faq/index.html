F.A.Q.

How do I add a line?

In manual mode you can mark a few points, and the app will create a smooth line using then. Find the object with the cursor in the editor, then tag a [+] plus button to add the point. Scroll video using the scrollbar below the editor.

Can Ace Trace do it automatically?

We’re working on it right now, we have some good results already. For your smooth experience the algorithm needs to be polished a little bit, so please be patient.

Do I need to buy it?

You can play with the app and see the results completely for free. In order to save resulting video to your gallery you need to buy a subscription. We offer 1 week free for you to try it out.

Why is there a subscription and not just a one-time purchase?

We have a variety of recurrent expenses, for example:

  • Renting servers that we use for improving video processing algorithms
  • 3d party cloud services

Our values:

  • Having continued income helps us keep working on adding new features and improving the app.
  • As we try to keep price very low, we cannot afford to make it free. Free products made by big companies usually sell user’s data for advertising.
  • When a product is free, you are the product. We have no intention of selling client’s data since we consider that being evil
  • We won’t add ads into the app, since we believe users must enjoy a good product without being bombarded with annoying banners.

Thank you for your understanding, your support is very important for us!

Do you have free options?

We do offer free subscriptions for school students or people with low income in certain cases. Feel free to send us a message describing your situation.

Are there any limitation?

Some videos have a black frame in the beginning (perhaps they were edited in some other software). These videos cannot be properly synchronized (compensating for camera motion). Symptoms: line is completely invisible even in the preview in the editor. Try removing that first dark frame using a trimming software such as iMovie

IOS

  • Right now 4K videos are not fully supported due to bug in IOS 14.5

Android

  • Only certain (standard) video dimensions work due to Android video codec limitations
  • Sometimes first 10-20 frames of a video might not be scrollable in the app if the video was trimmed in some other software. Try playing around with trimming and see if it works
  • In some videos a line is out of synch with the video (in the exported Ace Trace video it starts before the throw). Try resampling the original video to a different format or just use other videos.
  • Video codecs fail for variety of reasons on different platforms, and we cannot do anything about it - it’s the device manufacturers and Android platform who should fix these errors. Sometimes the best solution is to just upgrade your device.
