F.A.Q.

How do I add a line?

In manual mode you can mark a few points, and the app will create a smooth line using then. Find the object with the cursor in the editor, then tag a [+] plus button to add the point. Scroll video using the scrollbar below the editor.

Can Ace Trace do it automatically?

We’re working on it right now, we have some good results already. For your smooth experience the algorithm needs to be polished a little bit, so please be patient.

Do I need to buy it?

You can play with the app and see the results completely for free. In order to save resulting video to your gallery you need to buy a subscription. We offer 1 week free for you to try it out.

Do you have free options?

We do offer free subscriptions for school students or people with low income in certain cases. Feel free to send us a message describing your situation.

Are there any limitation?

Right now 4K videos are not fully supported due to bug in IOS 14.5
