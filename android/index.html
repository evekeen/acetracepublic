Ace Trace on Android

The good news - the Ace Trace app is available for Android.

Currently, the Free Curve and Golf editing modes are fully supported. The golf mode has fully functioning automatic tracking! You can start tracing your lines immediately.

Check it out!

While we are actively developing new features!

Limitations of the app on the Android platform

Android

  • Only specific (standard) video dimensions work due to Android video codec limitations
  • Video stabilization is slightly slower on Android due to the inability to read a video file in several parallel threads, unlike iOS.
  • Distance editor is not available on Android (we’re working on that)
